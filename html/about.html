About Sip & Serve Consumables

Your trusted supplier of high-quality coffee shop and beverage consumables.

Who We Are

Sip & Serve Consumables is an e-commerce platform designed to provide coffee shops, milk tea businesses, cafés, and beverage entrepreneurs with reliable access to quality consumable products. Our goal is to make ordering fast, convenient, and secure while providing a wide variety of products for different business needs.

Our Products

Plastic Cups

Available in Grecoo, Dabba, and Local brands with multiple sizes for cold beverages.

Paper Cups

Double-walled hot coffee cups available in various colors and sizes.

Lids

Strawless lids, coffee lids, dome lids, and flat lids compatible with different cup sizes.

Straws

Thin drinking straws and boba straws suitable for various beverages.

What Our Website Offers

  • Browse products by category
  • Search and filter products
  • Secure customer registration and login
  • Shopping cart and online checkout
  • Receipt generation after purchase
  • Order tracking and history
  • Real-time inventory updates
  • Responsive shopping experience

Our Mission

To provide businesses with affordable, quality beverage consumables while delivering a convenient and efficient online shopping experience supported by reliable inventory management and secure transactions.
